Let’s Play Tasmanian Games

A showcase of Tasmanian games—including released games and works in progress.

Date:

Friday, April 6, 2018

Come along to TasGM’s first showcase/demo event where you can come and play games developed by the community (and bring your own along too). This is a great chance to chat to other creators in Hobart, get feedback, and play a bunch of locally-made games!

We have four games lined up already (and we welcome even more!) :
I am Atlas - Ben Evans
Party Crashers - Giant Margarita
Panoptiformer (or Corpses Coil) - Shannon Woolley
Untitled game - Andrew Mendlik

If you have a game (in whatever form) that you would like to show, feel free to bring it along on the day. Get feedback from others in a supportive environment - it doesn’t mater if it is rough or unfinished!
