Jackbox Party Nights

Chris at Smash Attack Aus will be hosting nights of Jackbox games—and you’re all invited to join in on the fun!

Date:

Friday, May 22, 2020

This is a great chance for our community of Tasmanian game makers to come together and socialise during this period of social isolation—and what better to come together around than an online video game?

Jackbox Games are the creators of fun party games that you can jump in on by using your smartphone. It includes favourites such as Drawful, Quiplash, and the Jackbox Party Pack Series.

Up to 8 players can participate at once, but up to 10,000 audience members can also join in by voting on their favourites as the games play out. You can learn more about how to play here. The best part is you don't need an account to play along.

Next session

We’ll be jumping on to twitch.tv/smashattackaus at 7:30pm (AEST) this Friday 22nd May to have some laughs and play remotely, and we’d love to see you there.
