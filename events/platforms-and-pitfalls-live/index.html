Platforms and Pitfalls Live

Come along and listen to a live recording of Platforms and Pitfalls.

Date:

Wednesday, May 1, 2019

Come along and listen to a live recording of 'Platforms and Pitfalls'! This podcast takes a new game-design specific topic each month and discusses it within the context of different games.

TasGM hosted a live recording of Platforms and Pitfalls at the Enterprize co-working space in Hobart, Tasmania. Audience participation and suggestions were encouraged, and the event was live-streamed for those wanting to participate online. You can watch an archive of the event here!

Platforms and Pitfalls is run by Rowan and Adam, who both can't stop talking about the approaches, theory, and experiences of games.
