TasGM Lightning Talks (FutureFest)

As part of FutureFest 2019, Tas Game Makers (in partnership with ACS) is hosting a series of lightning talks.

Date:

Thursday, November 14, 2019

As part of FutureFest 2019, Tas Game Makers (in partnership with ACS) is hosting a series of lightning talks.

Come along and listen to a range of short talks by the TasGM community! For those unfamiliar, a Lightning Talks session is a series of short (+/- 5 min) talks by different speakers, in very quick succession. Talks will cover skills, experiences, or perspectives related to game development and will come from members of the Tasmanian game making community.
This is a free event, and a great opportunity to share experiences and learn from other skilled creators in the industry!

Hobart TAS 7000

If you have a topic that you’d like to speak about, we’d love to hear from you! 
The five minute format is designed to be as accessible and stress-free as possible; there will be a projector for slides if needed, but they aren’t required.

Special guest, Jane Stapleton from Screen Tasmania will be joining us at the networking session to talk about industry development opportunities for the games makes in the screen industry.

Drinks and food provided.
